JP
先輩に頼らなく、自分でミルクサービスが
できて、マサルくんは仕事に慣れるように
なった！成長を見守ってる店長が泣いて
喜んでくれた。全国大会を目指して、
予想外のナンバーワン給仕になりそう。

EN
Masaru-kun no longer relies on his senpai,
performing milk service all by himself.
He finally got used to his job! The shop manager
who watched over his growth is cry tears of
happiness. Masaru-kun could take aim at the
national convention and maybe become
the dark horse of waiter boy competition.
